Retro games are some of the most interesting pieces of software I've ever seen. Back in the day of the NES or ATARI there were no powerful engines to provide an easy development experience. Nor were there powerful processors capable of speeds higher than even 50Mhz. The way that early developers had to scrape as much power as they could to create the games they did is remarkable. I believe that if I could begin to understand this level of resource management. I could apply this knowledge to scrape precious seconds off more bloated processes in languages like Javascript and become a significantly better developer.

I love riddles and a website dedicated to such is simply too good to pass up!
